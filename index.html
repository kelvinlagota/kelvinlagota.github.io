I have completed all the requirements for my PhD last December 2019 and the degree was conferred last May 2020. Right now, I am sending my applications for teaching or research or postdoc positions to several institutions and I am hoping to receive at least a call or an offer.

My current research interest is in the field of Geometric Analysis and Spectral Theory. Specifically, I am interested in studying symmetric operators (e.g. Laplace operators) on manifolds (e.g. Riemann surfaces) with singularities and their spectral properties. I am interested in how some spectral invariants of an operator, such as the determinants, depend on the geometric properties of the manifolds.

My other interests lie in the fields of Functional Analysis, Complex Analysis, and Mathematical Physics (my newest/latest interest).
